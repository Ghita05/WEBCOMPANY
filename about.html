Who We Are

Founded in 2010, StratEdge Consulting began as a boutique financial advisory firm in New York. Today, we've grown into a full-service business consulting firm with offices in three countries, serving clients across industries and geographies.

What sets us apart is our integrated approach. Unlike traditional consulting firms that specialize in narrow areas, we combine financial expertise with strategic branding to deliver comprehensive solutions that drive measurable results.
